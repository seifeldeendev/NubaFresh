    • Traceability System

    As quality and efficiency is ensured through the use of full traceability system in fresh fruits and vegetables supply, we have a full documented tracing process that starts from seeds and ends by delivering the product to end customersâ€™ hands.

    We are able to conduct Forward or Backward traceability for all our supplied fruits and vegetables through the use of a label that is placed on the final product carton box. This label refers to the packed variety and the lot number.

    The lot number placed on the final packed carton box includes farm code, packing house Code and packing date and by using this lot number we can go back to related field plots and sub-plots to have our traceability details.

    For Pesticides traceability in our fresh produce, we can trace the applications of pesticides easily through a group of documented procedures that are included in our Integrated Pesticide Management system.
